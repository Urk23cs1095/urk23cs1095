            <p><b>Thangaiah Sweets</b> is a well-known sweet shop in <u>Tirunelveli</u>, specializing in traditional Tirunelveli sweets, particularly Muscoth Halwa. Founded in 1977 by Mr. Thangaiah Nadar in Thisayanvilai, the shop is recognized for its commitment to quality and hygiene in halwa production, using traditional methods.
